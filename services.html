Free Shipping & Returns

We offer free, safe no-contact delivery and free returns with every order, no matter how big or small. Orders usually deliver within 3-11 business days with our carrier. Shipping times may vary during the holiday season.

We will try to ship our your order as soon as possible, most likely within the first 48h, although this happens much faster depending on which time of the day the order has been placed.

Please note that the above delivery time starts after we have arranged the shipment, so please allow up to 48h for us to prepare the shipment in the cases when the order is placed in weekends, national holidays, etc.

We ship from Egypt as global hubs for best delivery services, this allows us to offer competitive prices and avoid overcharges.






Returns & Refunds

If you’re not totally satisfied with your purchase you can return new and unopened products within 14 days, together with your proof of purchase, for a full refund. You may also return open products within 14 days, with your proof of purchase, for a full refund.

Removal and Pick-up services at customers’ homes vary by market. If the Pick-up service is available in your area, please bring your product to your covered porch, garage or doorstep.

We do not accept returns on plants, cut fabric, custom countertops and as-is products. We are unable to refund or exchange your items if your merchandise is found to be modified from its original form when purchased, dirty, stained, or damaged. We apologize for any inconvenience.
